# Terminology (public-safe)

## Fractional leadership
Part-time executive leadership support (e.g., finance/accounting/HR) provided for a defined period or cadence.

## Interim leadership
Time-bound leadership coverage during a transition (e.g., backfill, transformation, integration).

## Quality of Earnings (QoE)
An analysis used in transactions to assess the sustainability and accuracy of earnings, often used in buy-side and sell-side diligence contexts.

## Exit readiness
Work done prior to a sale or ownership change to identify and address risks, improve reporting/controls, and reduce surprises in diligence.
