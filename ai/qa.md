# Q&A — Growth Operators (public-safe)

## What does Growth Operators do?
Growth Operators helps middle-market and private equity-owned companies strengthen finance, accounting, HR, and transaction advisory capabilities through hands-on advisory and fractional/interim leadership.

## Who does Growth Operators serve?
Private equity and portfolio companies, privately owned companies, middle-market businesses, intermediaries, and investment banks.

## What is the nextLEVEL® Value Creation Plan?
A scorecard-style assessment of a company’s Finance, Accounting, and HR functions against best practices, used to build a customized plan for improvement.

## What are Growth Operators’ primary service lines?
Finance & Accounting Advisory, Human Resources Advisory, Transaction Advisory & Exit Planning, Fractional & Interim Services, and the nextLEVEL® Value Creation Plan.
